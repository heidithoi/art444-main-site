About

Heidi Thoi was born and raised in San Diego, California. She is currently an undergraduate student at San Diego State University. She is majoring in Art with emphasis in Multimedia and minoring in Business Marketing. She recently began interning at Electronic Printing Solutions as a Graphic Design and Printing Intern. As she is graduating soon, the fields she is most interested in finding a career in are Multimedia, Graphic Design, and Web Design. Her ultimate goal is to contribute to society as a creative.
